Hayao Miyazaki's first full-length anime film, released by him in 1979, even before the creation of Studio Ghibli, was Lupine III: The Castle of Cagliostro. This is an example of total animation and the second full-length film about Lupine III, the hero of the animated series of the same name.
The latest film released by Hayao Miyazaki at the moment was “The Boy and the Bird”. The film premiered in Japan on July 14, 2023. The film participated in many international film festivals. The film was shown both in regular cinemas and in other premium formats such as IMAX
